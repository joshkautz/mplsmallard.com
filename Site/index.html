About Mallard

Mallard has been representing Minneapolis Men's Ultimate since 2018. The program has a heavy focus on growing ultimate in Minnesota, and has already grown to have two teams, Mallard I and Mallard II. Our program has qualified for Regionals every year, finishing 9th in 2018, 5th in 2019, and 3rd in 2021 & 2022. Our teams travel for high-level competition, having been invited to elite-select flight tournaments on multiple occasions. We even sent two teams to the U.S. Open in 2021!

Contact the coaches/captains at minneapolismallard@gmail.com.
